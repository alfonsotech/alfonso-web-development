About Me

I am a self-taught Front-End Web Developer with a background in Photography and Philosophy.


For the past five years, I have been running my own real estate photography business, working for brokers with outfits like Zephyr Real Estate, Climb Real Estate, Coldwell Bankers, Alain Pinel, and more. I have also worked as an Airbnb photographer in the United States, Canada, Mexico, and the Dominican Republic. You can see my photography portfolio here.


Prior to starting my photography business, I taught Philosophy and Gender Studies at U.C. Berkeley, Grinnell College, and Stony Brook University. My academic C.V. can be downloaded here.
